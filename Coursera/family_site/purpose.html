Purpose

One day during the Holiday Season/Pandemic/stupidity that is 2020 I had an epiphany. I no longer wanted to spend my days driving around in the elements, fighting through the unwashed masses to keep supermarkets stocked. I'm a fairly smart guy, I studied computer programming in college, why not take this opportunity to make a change and go back to school.

My lovely wife and I have goals of having an A-Frame cabin in the woods, that goal is not feasible if I have to work every weekend. I'd rather be able to sit in my home office and work remotely and give my family that goal.
